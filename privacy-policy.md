# Privacy Policy for FixHero Dev Inspector Chrome Extension

Effective Date: [Insert date]

FixHero Dev Inspector ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome Extension.

## 1. Information We Collect

We only collect limited data necessary for the functionality of the extension:

- **Website Content:** The extension captures selected webpage content such as text, images, links, and metadata to help you inspect and document issues.
- **User Activity:** The extension captures interactions such as element selections and clicks during inspection sessions.

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII), health information, financial data, authentication data, or personal communications.

## 2. Use of Collected Information

The information we collect is used solely for the purpose of:
- Allowing users to inspect web pages
- Generating issue reports and exports
- Enabling advanced features such as screenshots and annotations

We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored locally within the user's browser storage.
- Remote APIs (e.g., Firebase, OpenRouter) are used securely to process data, with no personal information transmitted.
- We implement security measures to protect your data.

## 4. User Control

Users have full control over their data:
- Data is stored locally and can be cleared at any time.
- No data is transmitted without user consent.

## 5. Compliance

We comply with the Chrome Web Store Developer Policies and applicable data protection laws.

## 6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on our designated page.
